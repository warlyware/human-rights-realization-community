Teaching Resources

The resources found here are a result of years of human rights study and countless hours of research. Through this lecture series, you can prepare human rights lectures and courses for your students. The lectures cover the foundations of human rights beginning with the Cyrus Cylinder, Enlightenment thinkers, the League of Nations, the United Nations, and the work of its specialized agencies. As our institution grows, we will continue to update this section with revisions and new content. The lectures currently found here were originally utilized as part of the Human Rights courses offered in the Humanities Department at Florida State University,
